Frequently Asked Questions

Is it really free to enter?

Yes! Every offer on WinItDaily is 100% free to enter. No purchase, payment, or credit card is ever required. If an offer page asks for payment, do not proceed and let us know.

Who can enter these sweepstakes?

Most offers are available to US residents aged 18 and older. Some offers may have different requirements — always check the eligibility details on the offer page before entering.

How does WinItDaily make money?

We earn a small affiliate commission from advertisers when you complete an offer. This costs you nothing — it's paid by the advertiser. This is how we keep the site free and running.

How will I know if I win?

Winners are notified directly by the advertiser via email. Make sure to check your inbox and spam folder regularly after entering. WinItDaily does not control winner selection or prize distribution.

How often are new offers added?

We update WinItDaily daily with fresh offers. Subscribe to our free email newsletter to get notified as soon as new giveaways go live.

Are these offers legitimate?

We only list offers from reputable affiliate networks. However, all sweepstakes are run by third-party advertisers and we encourage you to read the full terms and conditions on each offer page before entering.
